== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do you support WordPress 4.0? =

Yes, absolutely. Full compatibility.

= What charges are associated with your plug-in? Is it free? =

The basic plug-in is totally free. We also offer an enterprise-class version with powerful additional components. Please contact our team @ info@massadvertising.me for more details and to discuss your business case. 

= What type of Ad is this? =

Your Ad will be displayed in the form of an enterprise-class Captcha thus differentiating between humans and bots.

= Does Mass Advertising provide the advertising power? =

Mass Advertising is the exclusive partner of PlusCaptcha, who provides the advertising power behind the campaign.

= How can I contact you?  =

We offer 3 lines of communication.

info@massadvertising.me is for all general questions to our headquarters.

support@massadvertising.me is for all technical support questions.

We also offer a support line for all emergency related issues concerning the Captcha @ emergency@pluscaptcha.com

== Installation ==

Step 1: Download the Mass Advertising Plug-in

Step 2: Go to Mass Advertising Dashboard

Step 3: Click on the -Unique- button to create -New Account-. The process is seamless and takes less than 1 minute to complete. You will then be forwarded to the WordPress Dashboard (Note: if not, simply login again with your new credentials)

Step 4: Go to the -Mass Advertising- Dashboard and click on -external summary-. 

Step 5: Select your -advertising power-. (Note: Choose 1 and it's free)

Step 6: All Done! Go grab a coffee and run your business. Each MassAdvertising campaign undergoes a rigorous quality control and assurance check. This process may take a few hours and we will notify you once it is ready to execute.

Important Note: If you choose the -free campaign-, you will need to reactivate your campaign every 24hrs. This will prevent any unauthorized use or access to your site / blog.
